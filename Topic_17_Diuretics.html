Loop diuretics

Agents furosemide, bumetanide, torsemide; ethacrynic acid if sulfa allergy
Mechanism block Na-K-2Cl in TAL — most potent; also ↑Ca excretion
Uses CHF/edema, pulmonary edema, HTN with renal impairment, hyperkalemia, hypercalcemia (with saline)
SE hypokalemia, hypomagnesemia, hypocalcemia, contraction metabolic alkalosis, ototoxicity (rapid IV/with aminoglycosides), hyperuricemia, hypovolemia

Thiazides

Agents HCTZ, chlorthalidone, indapamide, metolazone
Mechanism block Na-Cl in DCT; retain calcium
Uses HTN (first-line), CHF, nephrolithiasis (↓urine Ca), nephrogenic DI
SE ("hyper-GLUC") ↑Glucose, ↑Lipids, ↑Uric acid (gout), ↑Calcium; plus hypoNa (esp. elderly), hypoK, hypomagnesemia; ↑lithium levels
Note less effective at eGFR <30 → use a loop

Potassium-sparing & MRAs

ENaC blockers (amiloride, triamterene) collecting-duct Na channel; uses: with loop/thiazide to spare K, Liddle, lithium-induced nephrogenic DI
MRA (spironolactone, eplerenone, finerenone) aldosterone-receptor block; uses: HFrEF (mortality), resistant HTN, primary aldosteronism, cirrhotic ascites
SE hyperkalemia (esp. with ACEi/ARB), metabolic acidosis; spironolactone → gynecomastia (eplerenone spares this)

Others

Acetazolamide glaucoma, altitude sickness, urine alkalinization; causes NAGMA
Mannitol ↑ICP, glaucoma; avoid in pulmonary edema/anuria
SGLT2 inhibitors mild osmotic diuresis + glycosuria; CV/renal protection
Vaptans (tolvaptan) V2-receptor antagonist — SIADH, ADPKD

Clinical use pearls

Diuretic resistance sequential nephron blockade — add a thiazide (metolazone) to a loop
Cirrhotic ascites spironolactone + furosemide (~100:40 ratio)
HFrEF loop for symptoms + MRA for mortality
Stone prevention thiazide reduces calcium stones (lowers urine Ca)

Pitfalls

Sulfa allergy loops & thiazides are sulfonamides → ethacrynic acid alternative
Loop vs thiazide & calcium Loops Lose Ca; thiazides reTain Ca
K direction loops/thiazides ↓K; K-sparing/MRA ↑K
MRA + ACEi/ARB monitor for hyperkalemia
Spironolactone gynecomastia + teratogenic — counsel
